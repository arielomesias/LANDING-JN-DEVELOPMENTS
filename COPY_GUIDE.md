# COPY_GUIDE.md

## Voice

Professional, direct, confident, helpful.

## Tone

- Clear
- Local
- Trustworthy
- Premium
- Action-oriented
- Not exaggerated

## Avoid

- Generic construction phrases
- Too much corporate language
- Fake urgency
- Unsupported guarantees
- Long paragraphs
- Overly technical explanations in the hero

## Copy Rules

Use short paragraphs.
Use bullets for scanning.
Lead with benefits, then explain services.
Speak to pain points before listing technical services.

## Strong Message Angles

- “Don’t let city paperwork delay your project.”
- “Know exactly what permits, plans, and reports your project needs.”
- “We coordinate the process so you don’t chase departments yourself.”
- “From ADUs to commercial projects, we help move approvals forward.”
- “Local LA & OC permit experience matters.”
