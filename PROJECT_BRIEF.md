## Audience

The landing page should speak to people who are frustrated, delayed, confused, or afraid of making mistakes in the LA/OC permit process.

## Emotional Drivers

Visitors likely want:
- faster approvals
- less paperwork
- fewer city corrections
- clarity
- someone who knows LADBS and local jurisdictions
- confidence before spending money on construction
