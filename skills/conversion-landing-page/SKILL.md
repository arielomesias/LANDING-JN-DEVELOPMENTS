# Conversion Landing Page Skill

Use this skill when creating or improving landing pages whose goal is lead generation.

## Workflow

1. Identify the primary conversion goal.
2. Identify the target audience and their pain points.
3. Build the page around one clear action.
4. Make the hero instantly understandable.
5. Put the CTA and form early.
6. Remove sections that do not support conversion.
7. Add proof, process, services, objections, FAQ, and final CTA.
8. Review mobile conversion flow.

## Rules

- Prioritize clarity over cleverness.
- Use short sections.
- Repeat the CTA naturally.
- Make the form easy to find.
- Use benefit-driven copy.
- Avoid generic filler.
