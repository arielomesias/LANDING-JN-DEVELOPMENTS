# CONVERSION_STRATEGY.md

## Landing Page Strategy

The page must be built for conversion, not just aesthetics.

## Primary Conversion

Form submission for a permit consultation.

## Secondary Conversion

Phone call to 310-405-3430.

## Hero Formula

Use this structure:

1. Problem-aware headline.
2. Clear promise.
3. Local credibility.
4. Short bullet benefits.
5. Form visible immediately.
6. Phone CTA.

Example direction:

Headline:
Get Your LA or OC Permits Approved Faster — Without City Delays or Costly Corrections

Subheadline:
JN Development handles permit expediting, plans, reports, and city approvals for ADUs, remodels, commercial projects, and multi-family developments across Los Angeles and Orange County.

Bullets:
- LADBS & local jurisdiction experience
- Residential and commercial permits
- Plans, reports, corrections, and approvals handled end-to-end
- Free consultation before you start

## Conversion Principles

- Keep the first screen focused.
- Do not overload the hero with too many services.
- Put the form above the fold on desktop.
- On mobile, show a strong CTA button that scrolls to the form.
- Repeat CTA after each major decision point.
- Make every service card lead back to the consultation.
- Use proof carefully. Do not invent testimonials or review counts.
- Avoid vague CTAs like “Learn More” unless secondary.

## Objection Handling

Address these objections:

1. “I don’t know what permits I need.”
2. “My project is stuck in plan check.”
3. “I’m afraid of delays or rejections.”
4. “I need plans and reports, not just expediting.”
5. “Can you help in my city?”
6. “How fast can this move?”
7. “How much does it cost?”
