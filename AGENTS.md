# AGENTS.md

## Project Goal

Build a high-converting landing page for JN Development, a permit expediting and project services company serving Los Angeles and Orange County.

The main conversion goal is: visitors submit the permit consultation form or call 310-405-3430.

This is not a generic corporate website. It is a direct-response landing page for homeowners, contractors, developers, and commercial property owners who need permits, plans, expediting, or project management.

## Primary Conversion Objective

Every section must move the visitor toward one of these actions:

1. Submit the consultation form.
2. Call 310-405-3430.
3. Understand quickly that JN Development can help with LA/OC permits.

Do not add decorative sections that do not support conversion.

## Main Offer

Free permit consultation.

## Positioning

JN Development helps homeowners, contractors, and developers get permits approved faster and with fewer mistakes across LA and Orange County.

Core promise:
“Permits approved faster, without city delays, paperwork confusion, or costly rejections.”

## Target Audience

- Homeowners building ADUs or remodeling.
- General contractors who need permit support.
- Developers working on multi-family or commercial projects.
- Property owners stuck in LADBS, LA Planning, LA Housing, or Orange County city approvals.

## Required CTAs

Use these CTAs throughout the page:

- Get Free Permit Consultation
- Start My Permit Process
- Call Now: 310-405-3430
- Request a Permit Review

## Source of Truth

Use `CONTENT_SOURCE.md` as the source of business information.
Do not invent licenses, addresses, reviews, guarantees, or statistics unless they are explicitly listed in the source files.

## Design Direction

Create a premium, modern, trustworthy landing page.
Style should feel like:
- construction consulting
- architectural plans
- city permit expertise
- premium B2B/B2C service
- professional LA/OC local authority

Avoid childish icons, generic stock-photo feel, and excessive animations.

## Technical Preferences

Use clean, maintainable code.
Prefer semantic HTML.
Prioritize mobile-first design.
The form must be visible above the fold on desktop and easy to reach on mobile.
Use smooth but lightweight animations.
Do not sacrifice performance for animation.

## Conversion Rules

- Hero must include headline, subheadline, trust bullets, phone number, and form or form CTA.
- Form should request only necessary fields.
- Phone number must be visible in header and hero.
- CTA must appear after every major section.
- Make the page skimmable.
- Use benefit-driven copy, not just service lists.
- Emphasize speed, reduced delays, fewer corrections, local expertise, and end-to-end handling.

## Sections Required

1. Sticky header with phone CTA.
2. Hero with form.
3. Trust bar / proof metrics.
4. Pain points.
5. Core services.
6. Project types.
7. Process.
8. Why choose JN Development.
9. Service areas LA + OC.
10. FAQ.
11. Final CTA with form or form anchor.

## QA Before Finishing

Before finalizing changes, check:
- Mobile layout.
- Form visibility.
- CTA consistency.
- Page speed.
- Accessibility.
- No fake claims.
- No broken links.
- No lorem ipsum.
